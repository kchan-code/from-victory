At produce time the engine drains KC's curation-inbox.md first, then searches the beat, picks a real, verifiable moment, writes the take in the operator's voice, credits the creator, and cites the source (`curationSource`). Link, react, quote with credit. Never re-upload someone else's footage. The 2026 World Cup (through the July 19 final) and August college camps are the near-term feed.
>
> Lane key: **CU** curate · **CR** create · **PE** personal · **SELL** sell. No streak content, no manufactured urgency, no emojis, no em-dashes. Final house-voice pass before anything ships.
