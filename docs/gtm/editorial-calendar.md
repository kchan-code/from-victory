# From Victory — Editorial Calendar (First 60 Days)

*Dated: 2026-06-22 · Weeks 1-8 · Tied to launch campaign phases (campaigns/launch-2026.md) · Channels: Instagram Reels + TikTok (cross-post), YouTube Shorts/long-form · Voice: kc-voice + company flavor*

> **Shape of the calendar.** The 90-day goal is first paying users, and content's job is to warm the waitlist, give partners a live feed to point at, and seed the category language. Pre-launch (wks 1-4) weights **Pillar 1 (Identity)** to build the audience with the most shareable format. Launch (wks 5-8) weights **Pillar 3 (Believing athlete)** and **Pillar 2 (pregame session)** to convert the warm waitlist and activate the rails. Two answer-engine anchors ship in Week 1 (the pregame protocol and the comparison article) because their citation window is the most time-sensitive thing in the plan.
>
> **Cadence.** ~3-5 short-form posts/week (IG Reels + TikTok, cross-posted) + 1-2 YouTube Shorts or one longer-form piece/week. Batch-film weekly to protect the founder's partnership hours (campaign Section 5). No streak content, no manufactured urgency, no emojis, no em-dashes. A final kc-voice pass runs before anything ships.
>
> Format key: R = Reel/TikTok · S = YouTube Short · TH = talking-head · TO = text-overlay · C = carousel · LF = long-form/article. AEP = answer-engine play (targets a citation/search cluster).

## How the calendar weights the funnel

- **Weeks 1-4 (pre-launch):** ~45% Pillar 1 (build the warm audience with the shareable reframe), ~35% Pillar 2 (plant the citable rhythm anchors), ~20% Pillar 3 (warm gatekeeper + parent). Two answer-engine anchors ship Week 1.
- **Weeks 5-8 (launch):** the mix shifts toward Pillar 2 (sport-by-sport pregame-session demos that drive trial-start relevance) and Pillar 3 (leader and parent conversion), because the job moves from reach to install and first-cohort conversion.
- **Every week** carries at least one pregame-session piece feeding the pregame answer-engine cluster, so the citable anchor compounds across the whole 60 days rather than being a one-time post.

## Distribution note

This calendar is the publish layer. How each piece travels beyond publish (cross-post, creator gifting, leader-kit repurpose, off-site placement) is in `content/plan` distribution and the campaign's channel mix. The single highest-leverage repurpose: the best-performing Reset Reels become the assets inside the huddle-leader kit (fv-006), so content work doubles as partnership ammunition.
